Welcome to the thrilling challenge of Simon!

Simon: Prove yourself as the ultimate champion by conquering Simon's electrifying puzzles!

Here are the rules you must follow:

  1. The game comprises four colored buttons: green, red, yellow, and blue.

  2. Your task is to repeat the sequence of colors created by Simon, pressing the buttons in the exact same order.

  3. With each successful repetition, Simon will add another color to the sequence, making the game increasingly challenging.

  4. Beware! If you press the wrong button and make a mistake, the game will come to an end.

  5. Remember, you must rely solely on your memory and not use any visual cues to assist you.

  6. Your goal is to achieve the highest score possible by creating the longest sequence without making any errors.

Are you ready to take on this battle of wits and prove your mastery over Simon's challenge?
